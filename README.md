# China Beauty Market Analysis

### K-Beauty vs C-Beauty Consumer Perception Analysis and CBE 2025 Market Observation

## Project Overview

This project analyzes global consumer perceptions of K-Beauty and C-Beauty brands using Reddit user comments and combines the findings with observations from Shanghai China Beauty Expo (CBE) 2025.

The objective of this project is to understand the changing competitive landscape of the Chinese beauty market and identify strategic implications for Korean beauty brands.

---

## Research Background

As Chinese beauty brands continue to gain market share, understanding consumer perceptions and market trends has become increasingly important for Korean beauty companies.

This project explores:

* How consumers perceive K-Beauty and C-Beauty brands
* Key discussion topics and sentiments
* Emerging beauty industry trends observed at CBE 2025
* Strategic implications for K-Beauty companies

---

## Data Source

* Reddit beauty-related communities
* K-Beauty comments
* C-Beauty comments
* Shanghai China Beauty Expo (CBE) 2025 field observations

---

## Methodology

### Text Mining

* Word Frequency Analysis
* TF-IDF Analysis
* Sentiment Analysis
* LDA Topic Modeling
* Network Analysis

### Machine Learning

* Naive Bayes Classification

---

## Key Findings

### Consumer Perception

* K-Beauty is strongly associated with skincare quality and innovation.
* C-Beauty is increasingly recognized for affordability and local market adaptation.

### Industry Trends

Observed at CBE 2025:

* Growth of functional skincare products
* Expansion of anti-aging solutions
* Rising popularity of biotechnology-based ingredients
* Stronger positioning of domestic Chinese brands

---

## Strategic Implications

### For K-Beauty Brands

* Strengthen premium positioning
* Focus on differentiated functional ingredients
* Avoid direct price competition with local brands
* Expand innovation-driven product development
